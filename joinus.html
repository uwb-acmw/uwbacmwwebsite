Why join UWB Association for Computing Machinary Women's Chapter?

Joining the UWB Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Women's Chapter presents a unique opportunity to thrive in the dynamic world of computing. By becoming a member, you open doors to a vibrant network of peers, mentors, and industry professionals, fostering connections that can profoundly shape your academic and professional journey. The chapter offers an array of enriching experiences, from skill-enhancing workshops and seminars to hackathons and coding competitions, providing practical avenues for honing your technical expertise. Moreover, you'll find a supportive community dedicated to empowering women in computing, fostering inclusivity, and bridging the gender gap in technology. Through this chapter, you'll not only gain access to invaluable resources and leadership opportunities but also find friendship, inspiration, and a sense of belonging, making your computing education a truly enriching and fulfilling experience.
