About ACM-W

ACM-Women (ACM-W) is a dedicated community within the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) that focuses on empowering and supporting women in the field of computing. As an integral part of ACM, ACM-W brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals, fostering an inclusive environment where women can thrive and make significant contributions to the computing industry.

Our mission is to support the professional growth of women in computing by providing opportunities for lifelong learning, career development, and professional networking. ACM-W creates space for mentorship, collaboration, and knowledge exchange, ensuring that women have the support and resources they need to excel in computing-related careers. Join the University of Washington Bothell's ACM Women's Chapter and be a part of a vibrant community that advocates diversity, equality, and excellence in the world of computing.
